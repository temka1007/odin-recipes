Mongolian daily dishes

  • Mongolian Steamed Dumplings (Buuz)

Buuz are filled with minced mutton or beef, which is flavoured with onion and/or garlic and salted.

  • Tsuivan
    •

    Tsuivan is an uncomplicated noodle stir fry made with rough-cut fresh noodles with fried meat and vegetables.

    • Fried Rice (Budaatai Huurga)

    Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.
