Creamy Garlic Alfredo Pasta

This dish is a rich and indulgent classic, known for its smooth and velvety texture. The Alfredo sauce, made from butter, garlic, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, coats the pasta in a luscious, creamy layer. The flavor is buttery with a hint of garlic and nutty Parmesan, creating a comforting and satisfying meal. When served, it often glistens with the creamy sauce, and a sprinkle of parsley adds a touch of freshness and color. It's the kind of pasta that feels fancy yet is incredibly simple to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 300g fettuccine or pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese, stirring until it melts and combines into a creamy sauce.
  5. Toss in the cooked pasta, adding a little pasta water if the sauce is too thick.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve hot!
