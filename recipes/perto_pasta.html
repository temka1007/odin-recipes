Pesto Pasta with Sun-Dried Tomatoes

This dish is vibrant and earthy, with a fresh basil pesto sauce coating the pasta in a bright green hue. The addition of sun-dried tomatoes adds a chewy, tangy sweetness that balances the nutty and herbaceous flavors of the pesto. The grated Parmesan cheese melts into the warm pasta, creating a savory undertone. The dish is colorful and visually appealing, with pops of red from the tomatoes and the option of pine nuts adding a pleasant crunch. It's a refreshing and flavorful meal that feels light yet satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 300g penne or pasta of choice
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pine nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water and drain.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sun-dried tomatoes and saut√© for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the pesto and cooked pasta, tossing to coat evenly. Add a splash of pasta water to help the sauce cling to the pasta.
  4. Mix in Parmesan cheese, season with salt and pepper, and garnish with pine nuts for a crunch.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy the fresh flavors!
