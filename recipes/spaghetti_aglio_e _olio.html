Spaghetti Aglio e Olio (Garlic & Oil Pasta)

This is the ultimate minimalist pasta dish, celebrated for its simplicity and bold flavors. The spaghetti is tossed in a fragrant sauce of garlic-infused olive oil, with just the right kick from red chili flakes. It’s light yet flavorful, with the garlic and chili providing a subtle heat and aroma. The texture is silky, thanks to the olive oil, and the spaghetti glistens in the golden sauce. Garnished with fresh parsley and a sprinkle of Parmesan (if desired), it’s a perfect dish when you want something quick, elegant, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley and grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water and drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic slices and sauté until golden and fragrant.
  3. Stir in red chili flakes and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat. Add reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  5. Season with salt, garnish with parsley and Parmesan, and serve immediately.
